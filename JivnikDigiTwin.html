JEEVNIK DIGITAL TWIN

Personalized Healthcare, Anytime, Anywhere

Jeevnik's Digital Twin is a virtual replica of a patient, built from their real-time health data, medical history, and lifestyle patterns. By continuously analyzing this data using AI and machine learning, our Digital Twin provides personalized health insights, helping both doctors and patients make informed decisions.

Currently Working on:
Insulin-Glucose Homeostasis Twin

Our first major focus is on developing a Diabetes Solution - the Insulin-Glucose Homeostasis Twin.

This AI-driven model will:

  • • Simulate glucose-insulin dynamics to help patients and doctors better understand metabolic health.
  • • Provide real-time monitoring and predictive alerts to prevent glucose fluctuations.
  • • Offer personalized lifestyle and medication recommendations based on individual metabolic responses.
